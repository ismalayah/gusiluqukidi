    Tyron Woodley is an American professional mixed martial artist and boxer.

    He is a former UFC Welterweight Champion who defended his title four times.

    Tyron Woodley parents: Sylvester Woodley and Deborah Woodley

    Deborah Woodley and Sylvester Woodley are the parents of Tyron Woodley.

    Deborah Woodley and Sylvester Woodley had 13 children and Tyron Woodley was the 11th child.

    Deborah Woodley single-handedly raised the children when her husband Sylvester left the family.

    She reportedly worked as a nanny and also took cleaning jobs at restaurants to raise money to take care of her children.

    Deborah Woodley in an interview with UFC on her children’s upbringing said; “I was a loving mom but I was tough. I had to be tough because I knew if I pamper them, all of them, if I pampered them and went along with all of that, they would use that as an excuse and I wasn’t having it”.

    “I didn’t want my son in trouble. I wanted to make sure that he had a good reputation and that his citizenship at school would be outstanding so that he can get a scholarship. I didn’t want him in the streets,” Deborah Woodley added.
