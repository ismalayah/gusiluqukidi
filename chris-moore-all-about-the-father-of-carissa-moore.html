    Divorce of Chris Moore from Carol Lum

    Chris divorced Carol Lum while his daughter Carissa was just 10 years old. It was a difficult period for their daughter at the time. She was suffering with her confidence and a body problem at the moment. She loves her father the most since he has played such an important part in her life. He has always been really supportive of her. Her parents remained close to her despite their divorce.

    Carissa Moore’s bio

    Nike, Red Bull, Target, Hurley, Subaru Hawaii, and many more companies support her.
