    1. Dragapult: What else can I ask for? This Dragon has my favorite type, Ghost , and we already know that if we combine Dragon with Ghost we only get amazing designs (Just look at Giratina).

    2. Goodra: It's without a doubt the most adorable of the group, and the anime just make me love it more . Such a shame that it didn't appear more .

    3. Hydreigon: As with Ghost, if you combine Dragon with Dark you can only get badass designs, and Hydreigon is a perfect example .

    4. Dragonite: Another cutie, I have to admit that I ignored it for a long time in favor of Dragonair until I used Dragonite in Unova, now I love the whole line. And Ash's Dragonite make me love it more.

    5. Garchomp: It has an amazing design but I have to admit that I was indifferent to it the first time I saw it, but I grew fond of it when I used it in Sinnoh. One of my favorites pokémon to use in battle.

    6. Salamence: An excellent pokémon to use in battle.

    7. Metagross: It has an amazing design but I like the others more, in fact I like its design better than Salamence's, but Salamence is higher because I prefer it in battle.

    8. Tyranitar: It has a good and intimidating design but is not my style.

    9. Kommo-o: I find its design overloaded, I love its dance in the games when it performs its Z-Move but it's the only Pseudo-legendary that I wasn't eager to use.
