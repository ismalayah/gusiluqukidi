    Two Island
    Location

    Location of Two Island in Sevii Islands.

    Two Island (Japanese: ２のしま Island 2) is the second of the towns in the Sevii Islands, located on Two Island, and one of the smallest. However, its value for players of FireRed and LeafGreen is far larger than its area. Its Market Stall is the only place where players can buy drink items in bulk, rare Poké Balls not produced in Kanto by Silph Co. (such as the Timer Ball), and several items only available in Johto and Hoenn (such as Moomoo Milk and Lava Cookies). In addition, RageCandyBars are sometimes available here, which Bruno visits the island for; however, in FireRed and LeafGreen, they are sold out, making Bruno leave empty-handed.

    Unlike in the town of One Island, the sea around the Seagallop Ferry dock is too rough to be Surfed on or fished in, and therefore, no Pokémon are available in the town itself.

    Places of interest

    Move Maniac

    There is also the Move Maniac in the town, who, in exchange for two TinyMushrooms or one Big Mushroom, will teach a Pokémon a move that it learns at a lower level, much like the man in Fallarbor Town in Hoenn will do for a Heart Scale. Next to his house is a small Game Corner that allows players to compete using their Pokémon in wireless minigames with another FireRed, LeafGreen, or Emerald game.

    Market Stall

    The Market Stall in the town changes its stock after certain game events have taken place.

    Joyful Game Corner

    Main article: Joyful Game Corner

    The Joyful Game Corner is a newly opened building on Two Island, of the Sevii Islands. It is owned by Lostelle's father, which can be played after the events at Three Island. This is where players from FireRed and LeafGreen can connect, along with Emerald, and can play multiplayer mini-games over the wireless communication system. Players of Emerald can connect to FireRed and LeafGreen and play the mini-games via a building in Mossdeep City. Up to five players from FireRed and LeafGreen and Emerald can play. There are two games that can be played at the Joyful Game Corner: Pokémon Jump and Dodrio Berry Picking. In Pokémon Jump, the player uses their Pokémon to jump rope while trying to keep in sync with other players' Pokémon. In Dodrio Berry Picking, the player uses a Dodrio to catch falling Berries from a tree. In order to increase the Trainer Card level, a player must score 2000 points on each mini-game.

    Demographics

    Two island has a population of 11.
