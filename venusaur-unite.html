    From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

    Venusaur (Japanese: フシギバナ Fushigibana) is a playable Pokémon in Pokémon UNITE. It was available as part of the base game when it released on July 21, 2021.

    It is an Attacker with powerful area of effect attacks.

    Abilities

    Passive Ability

    Overgrow
    しんりょく Fresh Greenery
    Description

    When HP is 30% or less, increase all damage dealt by 20%.

    Basic Attack

    Damage category: Physical (as a basic attack)
    Damage category: Special (as a boosted attack)
    Description

    Becomes a boosted attack with every third attack, dealing increased damage. This boosted attack also pulls enemies inward between the vines.
