    The Vampire Diaries was a supernatural teen drama television series that aired on The CW from 2009 to 2017. The show was based on the book series of the same name by L. J. Smith and followed the lives of Elena Gilbert (Nina Dobrev), a teenage girl who falls in love with two vampire brothers, Stefan Salvatore (Paul Wesley) and Damon Salvatore (Ian Somerhalder).

    The show was a huge success, and its cast became household names. Here is a look back at the main cast members of The Vampire Diaries and what they’re doing now:

    Nina Dobrev (Elena Gilbert)

    Dobrev plays the lead role of Elena Gilbert, a teenage girl who falls in love with two vampire brothers. Elena is a kind, compassionate, and selfless person who always puts the needs of others before her own.

    She is also strong, independent, and unafraid to stand up for her beliefs. After The Vampire Diaries ended, Dobrev went on to star in films such as Let’s Be Cops, xXx: Return of Xander Cage, and Flatliners.

    She also starred in the CBS sitcom Fam. In 2020, she launched her own production company, Fuller House Films.

    Paul Wesley (Stefan Salvatore)

    Wesley played Stefan Salvatore, the older of the two Salvatore brothers. Stefan is a good-hearted and compassionate vampire trying to make amends for his past sins.

    He is also a deeply conflicted character who constantly struggles between his human and vampire sides. After The Vampire Diaries ended, Wesley starred in the CBS All Access series Tell Me a Story.

    He also directed several episodes of The Vampire Diaries and its spin-off series, Legacies. In 2022, he launched his own production company, Outer Range Productions.

    Ian Somerhalder (Damon Salvatore)

    Somerhalder played Damon Salvatore, the younger of the two Salvatore brothers. Damon is a bad-boy vampire who is known for his dark sense of humor and his rebellious personality. He is also fiercely loyal to the people he loves.

    After The Vampire Diaries ended, Somerhalder went on to star in the Netflix series V-Wars. He also founded the Ian Somerhalder Foundation, an environmental organization that protects animals and the planet.

    Kat Graham (Bonnie Bennett)

    Graham played Bonnie Bennett, Elena’s best friend, and a powerful witch. Bonnie is a kind, caring person who is always willing to help her friends. She is also a strong and independent woman who is unafraid to stand up for her beliefs.

    After The Vampire Diaries ended, Graham went on to star in the Netflix film The Holiday Calendar. She also released her debut album, Love Life, in 2017.

    Candice King (Caroline Forbes)

    King played Caroline Forbes, Elena’s other best friend and a vampire. Caroline is a cheerful and optimistic person who always tries to see the best in people. She is also a fierce and loyal friend who is always willing to fight for the people she loves.

    After The Vampire Diaries ended, King starred in the Legacies, a spin-off series of The Vampire Diaries. She also released her debut single, “This Little Life,” in 2017.

    Other notable cast members

    • Steven R. McQueen as Jeremy Gilbert, Elena’s younger brother
    • Zach Roerig as Matt Donovan, Elena’s ex-boyfriend
    • Michael Trevino as Tyler Lockwood, a werewolf and Caroline’s ex-boyfriend
    • Joseph Morgan as Klaus Mikaelson, a powerful vampire and hybrid
    • Michael Malarkey as Enzo St. John, a vampire, and Damon’s friend
    • Matthew Davis as Alaric Saltzman, a vampire hunter, and Elena’s teacher

    The Vampire Diaries cast was one of the most popular and beloved casts on television. The actors and actresses brought their characters to life with passion and skill, and they helped to make the show a huge success.

    Conclusion

    The Vampire Diaries cast was a talented group of actors and actresses who brought the show’s characters to life in unforgettable ways. They were all essential to the show’s success, and their performances will be remembered for years.
